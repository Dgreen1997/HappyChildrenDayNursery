Providing EfficientEarly Education

We founded Happy Children Day Nursery in 2006 to provide better activities and early education programs for infants, toddlers, and preschoolers.

Our Nursery staff assist children who have typical needs as well as children with special needs, in order for them to learn and develop to their full potential. We maximize each child's cognitive, social, and daily living skills.
